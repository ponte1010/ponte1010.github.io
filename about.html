I Do What I love to do.

I want to solve social problems with the power of IT.

Currently, I am working to improve the learning environment for the visually impaired as one of the social issues. The progress of the learning environment of the visually impaired is delayed compared with the progress of the learning environment of non-handicapped people. Therefore, I would like to significantly update the learning environment for the visually impaired using state-of-the-art IT technology.
